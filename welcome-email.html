Sproutling's Learning Period

During the first 7 days Sproutling learns your baby’s sleep patterns and you can start seeing wake predictions (an in-development feature) after a week. It’s really important to log every nap–even when Sproutling isn’t worn–so it can better predict your baby’s wake up times.

Our insights are designed to improve over time and automatically adapt as your baby grows and changes.

The more you use Sproutling, the better results you’ll receive.
